How to choose brake pads for daily driving

For most daily drivers, quiet operation, low dust, and predictable stopping matter more than track-level bite. Start with your exact vehicle fitment and driving style.

Back to car storefront

Pick the right pad type

Ceramic pads are usually best for daily comfort and low dust. Semi-metallic options can give stronger bite but may add more noise and dust.

Always replace in pairs

Change pads on both sides of the same axle and inspect rotor condition at the same time to avoid uneven braking and vibration issues.
